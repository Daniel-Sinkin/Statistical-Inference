### 5.3
Let $X_1, \dots, X_n$ be iid random variables with continuous cdf $F_X$, and suppose $E X_i = \mu$. Define the random variables $Y_1, \dots, Y_n$ by

$$
Y_i =
\begin{cases}
1 & \text{if } X_i > \mu \\
0 & \text{if } X_i \leq \mu
\end{cases}
$$

Find the distribution of $\sum_{i=1}^{n} Y_i$.
